Outbreak

You lost!

Instructions

Imagine you were knocked out and kidnapped by a crazy mind-game-obsessed maniac. Once you woke up, you realized that you are locked in a room with no windows in it. There is a steel door that may be opened from the inside, but you need a secret password for that, which you don't know.

There is a piece of paper lying next to you and that turns to be the instructions from the maniac, who if offering you to play a game for your life. The game is simple. You need to figure out the digital key to open the room. This key is comprised of 5 unique digits 0-9. In other words, the digits will not repeat.

Once you start the game, you have only 12 attempts to guess the right password to open the door. While you are trying to guess - the room is filling with water and you need to hurry to get out and survive. You will see a timer showing how much time you have left to find the right combination. Once the timer hits 00:00 - you lose

The bad news is that you have 10*9*8*7*6 = 30,240 possible combinations. The good news is that after each attempt, you are offered some hints. Specifically, after each attempt you are being told how many digits you your guess were correct and placed well as "X" and also the number of those that were in the key, but are misplaced as "Y". You will not know which part of the key you got right - just the number of "X" and "Y". For instance, if the digital key is 51298 and your guess is 13259, then the hint you receive is 1X and 3Y.

The maniac was kind enough to close this letter by wishing you best of luck and suggesting there will be 10 doors like that on your way to exit. Each room will be more difficult to exit, but you will be always provided with a decent chance to survise. Obviously, the timer will always start over once you enter a new room.

Now, when you know what the game is about - put your mind to work and break free. If you get till the end, you will see a prize.
